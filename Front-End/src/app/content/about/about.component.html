About

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc is committed to providing patients with accurate and clinically significant test results. We implement rigorous internal and external quality control programs to ensure the highest level of testing accuracy.

With a highly trained staff capable of performing a broad range of tests, from basic to the most complex procedures, we offer free repeat testing if results do not align with a patient's clinical profile. We also provide flexible specimen pick-up times and reporting options to meet specific needs, with initial laboratory results available within 12 to 24 hours of specimen collection via fax or electronic transmission.

Alongside medical laboratory services, we are the top laboratory in the Chicago area for home and long-term care visits requiring blood draws and specimen collection.

We accept various insurance types, including those with high co-pays and deductibles, and offer a range of testing services to detect drug use or various medical conditions.

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc is licensed and accredited by several organizations, including the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CLIA), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (Medicare), and the Illinois Department of Health.
