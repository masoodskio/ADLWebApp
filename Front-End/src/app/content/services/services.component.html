Services

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc takes pride in providing excellent service to physicians, institutions, and patients. We respond to inquiries in a courteous and reliable manner and perform testing with accuracy and speed. Our personalized service is unmatched, and our field representatives have at least 8 years of experience in the industry. American Diagnostic Lab values transparency and provides full access to quality control records and results of our randomized proficiency testing. Interested individuals can call or visit the laboratory center for a full tour.

Courier Services

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc offers in-house courier services that prioritize the preservation of specimen integrity by efficiently retrieving patient specimens. We use advanced tracking systems to schedule pickup and delivery that can accommodate almost any pickup requirement.

House Calls

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc offers convenient home services and has a dedicated and empathetic staff to cater to the needs of home-bound patients. Our team of phlebotomists, who are supervised by experienced managers, is well-equipped to serve a wide range of client needs. Each phlebotomist can be easily located and dispatched when required. Additionally, all of the phlebotomists at American Diagnostic Lab, Inc have a minimum of 350 venipunctures under their belt.

Laboratory Results Delivery

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc provides flexible reporting options to ensure that reports are delivered when and how you need them. We offer various report delivery methods, such as courier service for daily delivery, customized options, immediate remote fax, an online laboratory management system, and the integration of their laboratory information system (LIMS) with your electronic medical records (EMR) or electronic health records (EHR).

Client Services

American Diagnostic Laboratory, Inc Client Services department is staffed by a team of professionals led by a dedicated supervisor with over twenty years of experience in client services. The department is operational for 10 hours a day, 5 days a week, and prioritizes the needs of each individual patient. We emphasize the values of courtesy, compassion, and professional expertise, and strive to provide accurate testing, prompt service, and responsiveness to clients' needs.
